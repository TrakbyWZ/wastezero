# Quick Start: Create or Verify Customers

Before creating sequences or batches, confirm the customer exists and is active.

---

## Why this step comes first

Customer setup is the foundation for sequence and batch configuration. If customer data is incorrect or inactive, downstream steps can fail or produce incorrect output.

---

## Process

1. Open **Customers**.
2. Search by customer number or customer name.
3. If customer exists, open and verify details.
4. If customer does not exist, create a new customer.
5. Save changes and confirm the customer appears correctly in the list.

## Fields to verify

- **Customer Number / ID**: Unique identifier used in operations.
- **Description / Name**: Human-readable name for operators.
- **Email / Contact**: Communication and traceability detail.
- **Active Status**: Must be active for operational use.

---

## Operator checklist

- Customer record exists or is created.
- Required fields are complete.
- Active status is confirmed.
