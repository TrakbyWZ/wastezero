# Quick Start: Login and App Navigation

This page covers how to access WasteZero and how to move through the app in the correct operational order.

---

## 1) Open the website

- Open your web browser (Chrome, Edge, Safari, or Firefox).
- Enter `https://wastezero-gold.vercel.app/` in the address bar.
- Press **Enter**.

You should see the sign-in page.

> **Tip:** If the page does not load, verify internet connection and URL.

---

## 2) Sign in

1. Enter your company email (for example, `jdoe@wastezero.com`).
2. Enter your password.
3. Click **Sign in**.

If credentials are valid, the app opens to the main operations area.

- If credentials fail, check for typing errors.
- If your account is inactive, contact your administrator.

![Login screen — enter your email and password, then click Sign in](/docs-images/help-login.png)

If your account requires password setup/reset, you may see this screen:

![Set a new password screen](/docs-images/help-login-new-password.png)

---

## 3) Follow the operations screen order

Use the top menu (or ☰ on smaller screens) and work in this sequence:

1. **Customers**
2. **Customer Sequences**
3. **Batches**
4. **Data Logs**

![Top menu: Batches, Customers, Customer Sequences, Data Logs](/docs-images/help-menu.png)
