# Quick Start: Sign Out and Other Options

After operations are complete, sign out and use available support options as needed.

---

## Sign out

- On desktop, use the **Sign out** control in the top-right corner of the app.
- On mobile or narrow screens, open the menu (☰) and use the **Sign out** option there.
- Confirm you return to the sign-in page after signing out.

## Display settings

- Use the theme control (sun/moon icon) to switch between **light mode** and **dark mode**.
- **Light mode** uses a bright background and is often best in bright rooms.
- **Dark mode** uses a darker background and can reduce glare in low-light environments.
- Theme selection only changes visual appearance and does not change system data or behavior.
